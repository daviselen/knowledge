# Davis Elen Glossary

> A shared reference for agency terminology, acronyms, and concepts.

## DE

The common internal abbreviation for Davis Elen.

## Creative Brief

A document that defines the strategic foundation for creative work, including objectives, audience, messaging, tone, and required deliverables.

## Account Team

The team responsible for managing client relationships, communication, scope, and project coordination.

## Retail Marketing

Marketing focused on driving consumer awareness, consideration, and purchase behavior for retail brands.

## OOH

Out-of-home advertising, including billboards, transit advertising, digital signage, and other physical placements.

## POS

Point of sale materials designed to influence consumer decisions at the purchasing location.

## ROAS

Return on ad spend.

## KPI

Key Performance Indicator

## CPM

Cost Per Thousand Impressions

## CTR

Click-Through Rate
