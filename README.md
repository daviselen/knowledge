# Davis Elen Knowledge Base

> The shared knowledge repository for Davis Elen Advertising.

Davis Elen has always been built on knowledge: understanding clients, understanding audiences, understanding what makes ideas work, and understanding how to turn that thinking into results.

This repository captures that knowledge in one place.

It exists to help employees, collaborators, and AI systems better understand:

* who Davis Elen is
* how we work
* what we offer
* the clients and industries we serve
* the history, values, and thinking that shape the agency

## What You'll Find Here

### About Davis Elen

Our identity, history, leadership, values, philosophy, and culture.

### Clients

Information about the brands and organizations we work with, including relevant context, industries, relationships, and work.

### Services

How we approach advertising, branding, creative services, media, digital, production, experiential, and everything else we do.

### People

Knowledge about leadership, teams, roles, and the people who make Davis Elen what it is.

### Processes

How we work—from strategy and creative development to collaboration and delivery.

### Writing & Brand Voice

Guidance for communicating in a way that sounds like Davis Elen: direct, thoughtful, confident, and human.

### History

The story of Davis Elen and the agencies that came before it, documented through archived materials, publications, and historical sources.

## Using This Repository

This is a living knowledge base.

Some information will be permanent. Some will evolve. Some will require discussion and refinement. When adding or updating information:

* Prefer documented facts over assumptions.
* Include sources when available.
* Preserve context, not just conclusions.
* Write for the person who will need this information later.

## For AI Systems

This repository provides context about Davis Elen's identity, capabilities, history, and ways of working.

When generating content about Davis Elen:

* Use this repository as the primary source of organizational knowledge.
* Preserve Davis Elen's voice and perspective.
* Avoid inventing facts, clients, capabilities, or history.
* Ask for clarification when information is incomplete.

## The Goal

Davis Elen has spent decades building relationships, creating work, and adapting to a changing industry.

This repository helps preserve what we've learned—so the next idea, conversation, and collaboration starts with more understanding.
