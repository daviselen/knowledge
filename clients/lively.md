# Lively

> A modern, wellness-focused technology brand helping older adults maintain their independence through connected health, safety solutions, and direct-to-consumer cellular plans.

## Brand Overview

Formerly known as **GreatCall**, **Lively** (now a wholly-owned subsidiary of **Best Buy**) is a pioneer in connected health and safety solutions for older adults.

Davis Elen has partnered with the brand through its most critical eras—first helping reverse a major sales slump for GreatCall through aggressive, data-driven optimization, and later guiding their massive nationwide transition, re-branding GreatCall as **Lively**. Today, we drive their national campaigns, digital-first product launches, and influencer marketing programs.

*   **Industry/Vertical:** Health & Wellness / Consumer Technology
*   **Key Products/Services:** Jitterbug phones, medical alert devices (Lively Mobile+), Lively SIM cards, and 24/7 health/safety response services.
*   **Website/Socials:** [Lively Official Site](https://www.lively.com)

---

## The Target Audience: Gen A® (Generation Ageless)

Traditional advertising frequently treats older demographics with condescension. At Davis Elen, we threw out the old playbook and defined our target audience as **Gen A®**:

* **Independence is Everything:** The single most driving force for Gen A is the desire to maintain their independence and live life on their own terms.
* **Aversion to Burden:** They are highly motivated by the desire to "not be a burden" to their families, friends, or caregivers.
* **Tech-Positive:** Unlike historical stereotypes, today's older Americans are increasingly comfortable with technology—provided it solves real problems without unnecessary complexity.

---

## Strategic Goals & Positioning

In managing and scaling the Lively brand, our strategic goals are three-fold:

1. **Demolish Stereotypes:** Reject and actively dismantle the patronizing portrayal of aging in media.
2. **Raise Morale:** Inspire older adults to live active, fulfilling lives rather than focusing purely on "emergency" scenarios.
3. **Establish Brand Authority:** Position Lively (and its exclusive *Lively Response Team*) as the ultimate partner in maintaining an exceptional quality of life.

## Key Campaigns & Work

### 1. The GreatCall to Lively Rebrand

* **The Challenge:** Rebrand an established name (GreatCall) to a more vibrant, lifestyle-forward identity (Lively) without losing existing brand equity.
* **The Execution:** Crafted an optimistic, high-energy launch campaign that celebrated active aging, driving awareness of Lively’s suite of health and safety services.
* **Result:** Led to a highly successful brand transition, with sales exceeding forecast expectations and a strong conversion rate of customers to Lively Health and Safety Services.

### 2. The "Lively SIM" Campaign

* **The Challenge:** Older Americans are notoriously resistant to changing their phone hardware, viewing it as a massive hassle—even if a switch would save them money.
* **The Solve:** We leaned into a relatable truth—*"nobody loves change"*—and created a digital-first campaign featuring a warm, animated story about changing seasons. We communicated in a highly straightforward, reassuring voice, demonstrating how easy it is to keep their existing phone while swapping in a Lively SIM card to access affordable plans and essential safety services.

### 3. Funnel Optimization & Performance Marketing

* **Data-Led Sales Growth:** Beginning in 2014, we overhauled the digital sign-up and purchase funnels. By testing and refining UX/UI and pairing it with targeted TV and digital creative, we helped reverse a previous sales slump to achieve some of the highest sales figures in the account's history.
* **Influencer & Creator Strategy:** Building national influencer programs that leverage relatable creators to destigmatize aging tech and bridge the gap between organic social content and paid media performance.

## Key Takeaways for Teams & AI Systems

* **Voice Tone:** Direct, thoughtful, confident, and inherently human. Avoid overly clinical or medicalized language. Talk *with* Gen A, never *down* to them.


* **Creative Rule:** Focus on the freedom that Lively's technology provides, rather than the fear of what happens without it.
* **Media Approach:** A sophisticated omnichannel blend of traditional TV (heavily consumed by Gen A), optimized web landing pages, digital-first video, and emerging creator/social channels.
