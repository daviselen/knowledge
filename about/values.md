# Davis Elen Core Values

> The core values that guide how Davis Elen approaches its people, partnerships, and work.

## Passion

**At work. For life.**

We bring energy, curiosity, and commitment to what we do. Great work comes from people who care deeply about their craft, their clients, and the impact of their work.

## Wisdom

**In our thinking. In our doing.**

We combine experience, judgment, creativity, and strategic thinking to make decisions that create meaningful results.

## Loyalty

**To our partners. To ourselves.**

We value long-term relationships—with clients, colleagues, and partners—and believe trust is built through commitment and consistency.

## Diversity

**In our people. In our work.**

We believe different perspectives create stronger ideas and better outcomes. Diversity of experience, background, and thinking strengthens our agency and our creative work.

## Trust

**The glue that holds it all together.**

Trust is the foundation of our relationships—with clients, colleagues, and partners. It enables collaboration, creativity, and accountability.

## Credo

> A statement of Davis Elen's belief in the power of advertising to create emotional connections between brands and people.

Great advertising does more than communicate information.

It creates a connection.

When work can:

* Make someone smile
* Make someone laugh
* Make someone cry
* Make someone think differently
* Reveal something about human nature
* Create a meaningful emotional response

Then it has achieved something valuable.

Advertising should speak to both the hearts and minds of its audience.

When it creates understanding, emotion, and connection, it has done its job well.
